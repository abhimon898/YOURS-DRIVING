Our Driving Course Activities

Day 1 - Introduction

Introduction to vehicle basics, traffic rules, road signs and safety instructions.

Day 2 - Vehicle Controls

Learn steering control, clutch, brake, accelerator and gear handling.

Day 3 - Starting & Stopping

Practice smooth vehicle starting, stopping and moving in traffic safely.

Day 4 - Gear Practice

Learn correct gear shifting techniques and speed control methods.

Day 5 - Turning & Parking

Practice left/right turns, reverse driving and parking skills.

Day 6 - Road Training

Drive on real roads with instructor guidance to improve confidence and safety.

Day 7 - Traffic Handling

Learn how to manage traffic situations, signals and busy road driving.

Day 8 - Hill & Reverse Practice

Special practice for hill starts, reversing and difficult road conditions.

Day 9 - Mock Driving Test

Full driving test practice with guidance and correction from trainers.

Day 10 - License Assistance

Final assessment and support for driving license test procedures.
